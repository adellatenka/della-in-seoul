home campus life pretty places concert diaries

i was really lucky to be able to see some of my favorite groups perform while i was in seoul.

here are the concerts i attended!

group: EXO

tour: Fan Meeting: ONE

date: april 14, 2024

venue: inspire arena


exo was the first kpop group i ever became interested in, almost 10 years ago. the members have been completing their military service, but they happened to hold a fanmeeting with the active members while i was in korea. it has been a while since they've toured in the u.s., but finally getting to see my first group was such an amazing experience.


the venue was in incheon, which was around 2.5 hours from my dorm, so it was pretty far!

tour: The Dream Show 3: DREAM()SCAPE

date: may 7, 2024
